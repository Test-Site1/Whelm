The Whelmbulance: Education on the Move

The Whelmbulance is a mobile education unit dedicated to bringing learning opportunities directly to children across South Africa. We drive to schools, communities, and rural areas, equipping kids with essential knowledge and skills.

Our mission is to ensure every child, no matter where they live, has access to quality education and the tools they need to thrive. Whether it’s helping with homework, introducing new subjects, or sparking curiosity, the Whelmbulance is committed to teaching and inspiring kids on the go.

Together, we’re making education accessible and empowering the next generation, one mile at a time.
