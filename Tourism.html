Welcome to Clanwilliam

Clanwilliam is a charming town located in the Western Cape, South Africa. With stunning natural landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture, it’s the perfect getaway for adventure seekers and relaxation lovers alike.

Things to Do and See

Explore the majestic Cederberg mountains, enjoy a boat ride on Clanwilliam Dam, or experience the local heritage at Clanwilliam’s cultural sites. No matter your interests, Clanwilliam offers something for everyone!
